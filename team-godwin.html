Rev. Godwin G.D. Ampony

About

Rev. Godwin Ampony brings over 20 years of experience in social justice and public health, with a strong focus on research in neglected tropical diseases. His commitment to health equity drives him to generate insights that can inform policy and strengthen community health outcomes across Ghana. Rev. Ampony is dedicated to fostering meaningful collaborations that bridge the gap between research and practice, and he believes that informed advocacy is key to building a healthier and more just future for all Ghanaians.
